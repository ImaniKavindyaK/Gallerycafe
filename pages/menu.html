WHY CHOOSE US

The Gallary Cafe is a limited liability company having registered its business in Sri Lanka in January 2020 and is gradually evolving as a popular Restaurant chain in Colombo serving a multi-cuisine menu to a wide range of customers. The Vision of Cuisine Colombo is to become a renowned chain of Restaurants with branches in different parts of the World serving top quality food with an array of dishes to cater to a majority. Given that Cuisine Colombo is a multi-cuisine restaurant, it caters to different pallets, Kids to Adults, Vegetarian to Non-Vegetarian preferences, Vegan dishes, those who prefer healthy food, etc. The Cuisine that is served includes Srilankan, Italian, Chinese and other items of foods as well.

ALWAYS TASTY MEAL

Come and enjoy our selection of lasagnas, pastas, daily special lunch, shorteats and desserts. Whether you are looking to relax over an ice coffee or grab a quick bite to go, our restaurant is for you!
